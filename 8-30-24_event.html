Event Information for 8/30/24 Boise/Portland Regional Event

The Pre-Registration Details for our Camp Arrowhead Game

Introduction

This is for the Refuge Boise/Portland 2024 Regionals event happening on Aug 30 - Sept 2, 2024 at Camp Arrowhead near Stevenson, WA!

We will be using the current provisional ruleset. Character Cards will be printed 2 weeks prior to the event right after registration closes (printing on Aug 17th)..

Registration is now OPEN! (If you have missed the early registration deadline please still send in a registration if you are planning on coming to the event. While you may not get the discount it still helps check-in run more smoothly). Registration deadline is Friday, Aug 16th, at 11:59 PM.

To register, please visit the event sign up page.

Pre-Registration Information

  • Price for Standard PC's is $120 and payment can be sent via:
    • Square: https://square.link/u/3pVzqyGU
    • Paypal: refugelarpportland@gmail.com
  • Are you PC'ing Refuge for the first time? Your price is $60 and payment can be sent via:
    • Square: https://square.link/u/TAbiOoNK
    • Paypal: refugelarpportland@gmail.com
  • Pre-Reg closes on Friday, August 16th, at 11:59 PM.
  • Payment preferred prior to the event.
  • We will have a scholarship fund. Contribute an event fee by purchasing one above (please specify that this is for the "scholarship fund" either when you send the payment, or via email after the fact, so we know who to credit! Thank you!) or by donating $120, and you will start the game off with a FREE FULL BOAT OF PROTECTIVES.

Game Info

  • PCs can be on site no earlier than 3:30pm on August 30.
  • Check-in Time: Start at 5pm
  • Game-on Time: Approximately 9pm, depending on how check-in goes
  • LCS MI Policy
    • Up to 20 LCS Rits allowed from any chapter. Exceptions are the standard Portland Exceptions (found here)
  • Character sheets will be printed on August 17th. Make sure your memorization lists are in place prior to registration closing.

General Reminders

Meal Plans

  • NPC Meal Plan
    • Expected Cost: $50
    • Complete the questions in the pre-reg if you wish to participate. We will email you the payment link when we have the meal plan details confirmed (if one is able to be offered). Payment preferred prior to the event.
    • We are still looking for people to run the meal plan. Reach out to refuge.regional.logistics.2024@gmail.com if interested in helping. (Chapter points awarded for this work.)
  • PC Meal Plan
    • Expected Cost: $50
    • Complete the questions in the pre-reg if you wish to participate. We will email you the payment link when we have the meal plan details confirmed (if one is able to be offered). Payment preferred prior to the event.
    • We are still looking for people to run the meal plan. Reach out to refuge.regional.logistics.2024@gmail.com if interested in helping. (Chapter points awarded for this work.)

Covid Policy

  • Proof of COVID-19 vaccine must be submitted via email to refuge.regional.logistics.2024@gmail.com by Aug 29 at 10pm.
  • Proof of a COVID-19 rapid test required within 24 hours of game-on (10 pm).
    • You will be asked to show proof of this negative test in the form of a picture of the negative test, the date (ideally written on the test in sharpie), and your face.
    • All three of these things must be in the same picture.
    • Alternatively, you are welcome to take the test on-site and show it directly to the person doing COVID screenings.
    • If you choose to do this, please bring your own test.
    • If, for any reason (including finances), you cannot acquire a rapid test, we will have a small number of rapid tests available with the person doing COVID screenings. Tests cost about $10 a piece, and if you use a test please consider making an equivalent donation if you are able to do so.
  • Please see our full COVID-19 policy for further details. Questions, comments or concerns may be directed to owner@refugelarpportland.com.

New Characters

If your character has not been played before, please complete a copy of the New Character Starting Gear document and email it to refuge.regional.logistics.2024@gmail.com. We will be unable to generate new character magic items for you at check-in and you will not be able to do this at a later date.

Conversions

If you have unconverted items please out to your local logistics or refuge.regional.logistics.2024@gmail.com to determine the best route for conversion.
