Contact Us

How to Reach Out

If you'd like to reach out for any reason, please feel free to do so! We are always happy to help answer questions, solve problems, and welcome new people to our fantastic hobby! We're crazy-active on our social media pages in particular.

Refuge LARP Portland Information

Discord

Our Discord Server is SUPER active and raring to go! We have events on there regularly, and it seems like someone is always around to answer questions and help solve problems. You are encouraged to start here, no matter what your needs and desires!

Facebook

Our Facebook page is not super active, but people are frequently online! If you have questions and want answers, feel free to post up there, and we'll be happy to respond. The closed group status is to deter bots. Don't be deterred yourself!

We use various social media platforms for our LARP. Our main area is Discord. It offers a very central location for chat, visual media, event planning and more. If there is one place you want to be, it is our Discord Server! Our plot teams run some online content there (less now that we're getting back to in-person gaming), we have IG and OOG chat spaces, and lots of ways to get to know people before you take the plunge and join us in person! Announcements will let you know what is coming up and when, so you can stay up-to-date on the important stuff.

Facebook is another great tool for our your use. Announcements are cross-posted there, so you won't ever miss out, and people are frequently online and happy to answer questions. It's not as active as our Discord Server, but it's still a good place to hang out.

Finally, we have the Refuge LARP Forums. These were built to support all of the Refuge Chapters, and our announcements are cross-posted there too! If you want to learn more about Refuge as a whole or any of our other Chapters, that's a great place to start!

Interested in more? Refuge LARP (the parent organization) has an Instagram, a YouTube Channel, and a Twitter. Feel free to give those a follow, if you're interested!

Refuge International Information

Instagram

We are just getting started with Instagram, but as we have more events, this will be the best stop to see what we do! Instagram

YouTube

We post all of our videos on YouTube. We try to limit videos as they can be rather distracting an immersion breaking, but some events, we truly get some gems! Subscribe Here

Twitter

Our least used social media. We usually forward other links or articles through twitter, but it is not our main focus. Follow us at your Peril!
