Meet Our Staff

The Staff of Refuge PDX

Barbara

Positions: Owner, Director, Head of Plot (Palla), NPC Guild (BroS), Rules Marshal, TP Manager, Discord Admin, Web Admin

Contact Email: owner@refugelarpportland.com, plot@refugelarpportland.com

Social Media: On Discord and Facebook - please DM me if needed!

Bio: Barbara (they/he/she) has been LARPing with Refuge Portland since 2009 - when it was still a part of its prior organization. They have both PC'd and NPC'd for years, and are currently absolutely enamoured with heading up plot for the Palla campaign. They are pretty sure there's nothing they like better than telling stories with other players - except maybe their spouse and child. Barbara has also served in other roles within both the Refuge and other local LARPs, like Logistics and NPC chef, and has been low-key obsessed with LARPing since they started playing. While they are new to being an owner, they think that their experience with the game and their incredible staff and player base will help give them the guideance needed to be successful in the role. Given the trials of the pandemic, Barbara is ecstatic to get back to LARPing regularly - although they enjoyed the online events that were run as well - and can't wait to resume the hobby that has been their passion for so long.
